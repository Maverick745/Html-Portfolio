My top 3 movies of all time

The Dark Knight

Christopher Nolan's The Dark Knight is a masterclass in superhero filmmaking. It's a dark, gritty, and realistic take on the Batman mythos that's unlike anything we've seen before. The film is anchored by an incredible performance from Heath Ledger as the Joker, who steals every scene he's in. The Dark Knight is a film that transcends the superhero genre and stands as one of the best movies of all time.

The Godfather

The Godfather is a masterpiece of cinema that tells the story of the Corleone crime family. The film is a sweeping epic that spans decades and explores themes of power, family, and loyalty. Marlon Brando gives an iconic performance as Vito Corleone, and the film is filled with memorable moments and quotable lines. The Godfather is a film that has stood the test of time and remains one of the greatest movies ever made.
